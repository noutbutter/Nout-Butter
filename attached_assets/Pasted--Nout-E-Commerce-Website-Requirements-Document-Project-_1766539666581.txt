# Nout E-Commerce Website - Requirements Document

## Project Overview
Build a clean, modern, and bold e-commerce website for Nout, a niche cognitive enhancement product. The site should create a lasting impression on consumers through striking design and seamless user experience.

## Design Inspiration & Brand Direction
Taking cues from: Nguyen Coffee Supply, The Cumin Club, Pika Pika, Yishi Foods

**Key aesthetic principles:**
- Bold typography with strong hierarchies
- Vibrant, memorable color palettes
- High-quality product photography with editorial feel
- Generous white space for breathing room
- Cultural authenticity and storytelling
- Premium yet approachable positioning

## Technical Stack Recommendations
- **Frontend:** React with Next.js for SEO and performance
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS for rapid, modern design
- **E-commerce:** Shopify integration or Stripe for payments
- **Hosting:** Vercel (seamless Next.js deployment)
- **CMS:** Sanity.io or Contentful for product content management

## Core Features & Functionality

### 1. Homepage
- **Hero Section:** Full-width, bold headline with high-impact product imagery or video
- **Value Proposition:** Clear, concise messaging about what Nout is and why it matters
- **Product Showcase:** Featured products with compelling visuals
- **Social Proof:** Customer testimonials, reviews, or press mentions
- **Educational Content:** Brief section on cognitive enhancement/product benefits
- **Newsletter Signup:** Prominent email capture for marketing

### 2. Product Pages
- **High-Quality Images:** Multiple angles, lifestyle shots, detailed close-ups with zoom capability
- **Product Information:**
  - Clear pricing with any subscription options
  - Detailed descriptions with benefits-focused copy
  - Ingredients/supplement facts panel
  - Usage instructions
  - Science/research backing (if applicable)
- **Add to Cart:** Prominent, friction-free purchase flow
- **Quantity Selector:** Easy to adjust order quantities
- **Reviews Section:** Customer ratings and written reviews
- **FAQ Accordion:** Common questions answered inline
- **Cross-sells/Upsells:** "You might also like" recommendations

### 3. Shopping Experience
- **Cart:**
  - Slide-out cart drawer (non-intrusive)
  - Easy quantity adjustments
  - Clear pricing breakdown
  - Progress indicators for free shipping thresholds
- **Checkout:**
  - Guest checkout option
  - Saved payment methods for returning customers
  - Multiple payment options (credit card, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay)
  - Address autocomplete
  - Order summary visible throughout
- **Subscription Options:** If applicable, recurring purchase options with savings

### 4. About/Story Page
- **Brand Narrative:** Why Nout exists, the founder's story
- **Mission & Values:** What the brand stands for
- **Product Development:** How products are created/sourced
- **Team:** Faces behind the brand (builds trust)
- **Visual Storytelling:** Mix of text, imagery, and potentially video

### 5. Blog/Educational Content
- **Articles:** SEO-optimized content about cognitive health, productivity, wellness
- **Categories:** Easy navigation by topic
- **Author Profiles:** Build authority and trust
- **Social Sharing:** Enable easy content distribution

### 6. Customer Account Area
- **Order History:** View past purchases
- **Subscription Management:** Modify, pause, or cancel subscriptions
- **Saved Addresses:** Quick checkout for repeat customers
- **Wishlist:** Save products for later

### 7. Mobile Experience
- **Fully Responsive:** Optimized for all screen sizes
- **Touch-Friendly:** Large tap targets, swipeable galleries
- **Fast Loading:** Optimized images and code splitting
- **Mobile-First Design:** Primary experience designed for mobile

## Design System Specifications

### Typography
- **Logo Font:** Roca Two
- **Body Font:** TT Chocolates (all body text, paragraphs, descriptions)
- **Headline Font:** TT Chocolates Bold/Heavy weights for headings (maintaining brand consistency)
- **Accent/Label Font:** TT Norms Pro (for labels, tags, navigation, buttons, pricing, and micro-copy)
  - Alternative: Graphik (if TT Norms Pro unavailable)
  - Rationale: TT Norms Pro shares the same foundry as TT Chocolates, ensuring cohesion while providing functional clarity for UI elements
- **Scale:** Clear hierarchy from H1 (48-72px) down to body (16-18px)
- **Line Height:** Generous (1.6-1.8 for readability)
- **Font Loading:** Ensure custom fonts are optimized (woff2 format) and use font-display: swap to prevent layout shift

### Color Palette
- **Primary Brand Color:** Bold, memorable (inspired by reference brands' vibrant choices)
- **Secondary Color:** Complementary accent
- **Neutral Base:** Warm whites/off-whites, sophisticated grays
- **Accent Colors:** For CTAs, highlights, success/error states
- **Ensure WCAG AA contrast compliance**

### Layout & Spacing
- **Grid System:** 12-column responsive grid
- **Max Content Width:** 1400-1600px for readability
- **Section Padding:** Ample vertical spacing (80-120px desktop, 40-60px mobile)
- **Component Spacing:** Consistent spacing scale (8px base unit)

### Imagery
- **Product Photos:** Professional, well-lit, consistent styling
- **Lifestyle Photography:** Real people using products in authentic settings
- **Image Optimization:** WebP format with fallbacks, lazy loading
- **Aspect Ratios:** Consistent across similar content types

### Interactive Elements
- **Buttons:**
  - Primary: Bold, high-contrast CTA buttons
  - Secondary: Outlined or subtle background variants
  - Hover states with smooth transitions
- **Animations:** Subtle micro-interactions (fade-ins, hover effects)
- **Loading States:** Skeleton screens or elegant spinners

## Performance Requirements
- **Page Load Speed:** Under 3 seconds on 3G
- **Core Web Vitals:**
  - LCP (Largest Contentful Paint): <2.5s
  - FID (First Input Delay): <100ms
  - CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift): <0.1
- **Image Optimization:** Compressed, properly sized, next-gen formats
- **Code Splitting:** Load only necessary JavaScript

## SEO & Marketing

### Technical SEO
- **Meta Tags:** Unique titles and descriptions for all pages
- **Structured Data:** Product schema markup for rich results
- **XML Sitemap:** Auto-generated and submitted
- **Robots.txt:** Properly configured
- **Canonical URLs:** Prevent duplicate content issues
- **Alt Text:** All images properly described

### Marketing Integration
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4 or Plausible
- **Email Marketing:** Integration with Klaviyo, Mailchimp, or similar
- **Social Media Pixels:** Facebook, Instagram, TikTok tracking
- **Conversion Tracking:** Goal and e-commerce tracking setup

## Accessibility Requirements
- **WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance**
- **Keyboard Navigation:** All interactive elements accessible
- **Screen Reader Support:** Semantic HTML, ARIA labels where needed
- **Focus Indicators:** Clear visual focus states
- **Color Contrast:** Minimum 4.5:1 for normal text
- **Form Labels:** Clear, associated labels for all inputs

## Security & Trust
- **SSL Certificate:** HTTPS throughout
- **PCI Compliance:** For payment processing
- **Privacy Policy:** Clear, accessible privacy documentation
- **Terms of Service:** Legal protections in place
- **Trust Badges:** Display security and payment badges
- **Customer Service Info:** Prominent contact information

## Content Requirements

### Copy Tone & Voice
- **Confident:** Assert the product's value without overselling
- **Clear:** Avoid jargon, explain benefits simply
- **Authentic:** Human, relatable language
- **Educational:** Inform rather than just sell
- **Inclusive:** Welcoming to all potential customers

### Product Descriptions
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Use sensory, evocative language
- Include technical specifications where relevant
- Address common objections preemptively

### Calls to Action
- Action-oriented (e.g., "Boost Your Focus" vs. "Buy Now")
- Create urgency when appropriate (limited stock, special offers)
- Clear value proposition in CTA text
